ABOUT US

Biba Baking Supplies Shop is a sole propriety owned and operated by Charish S. Tanawan. She has 11 years of extensive experience in baking and cake decorating industry having worked as the pastry chef and decorator of Biba Baking Supplies Shop. She attended Academy Asia School of Technology and the Arts for an extensive training in Baking and Pastries. She also attended several Master Classes by various experts in baking and cake decorating. She is an active member of Filipina Homebakers Online, a pioneer online community all female homebakers and Bakehappy Manila, a well-known online community for all bakers and cake decorators.
